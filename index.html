Biography

  • 2023-. I am currently employed as an Algorithm Engineer at Kuaishou, where my primary responsibility lies in the development of cutting-edge algorithms within the realm of computational advertising.

  • 2020-2023. I obtained my M.Sc. degree from the School of Artificial Intelligence at Nanjing University (NJU), where I am very fortunate to be a member of LAMDA Group and supervised by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou.
    My research interests include machine learning and data mining, especially:

    • weakly supervised learning
    • machine learning under online distribution shift

  • 2016-2020. I obtained my B.Sc. degree in the School of Computer Science and Engineering from Beihang University (BUAA) in June 2020, achieving a remarkable GPA of 3.86, which placed me in the top 1%. Then, I was admitted to pursue M.Sc. degree at Nanjing University without entrance examination.
